DevTrex

Empowering The Next Generation of Entrepreneurs

DevTrex is an entrepreneurship and business program that gives students the opportunity to accelerate into the business field. DevTrex is designed to maximize the potential of students and help them launch their very own startups. DevTrex is dedicated to enabling young entrepreneurs enhance their business skills and entrepreneurial prowess through its many competitions, seminars, and workshops. So far DevTrex has established 10+ chapters of which 2 are located in India. The DevTrex team continues to expand more than any high school or middle school, with students who are interested in learning about applications to business.

As part of yet another intiative, DevTrex provides free web design services for local businesses.
